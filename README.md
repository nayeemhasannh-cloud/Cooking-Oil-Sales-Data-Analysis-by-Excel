Supply Chain KPI Dashboard of Cooking Oil Sales Data (Excel)

Overview
This project presents a Supply Chain KPI Dashboard built in Excel to monitor and analyze key performance metrics such as demand, inventory turnover, lead time, and stockout rate. The goal is to identify inefficiencies and support data-driven decision-making in procurement and inventory management.

Objectives
- Track key supply chain KPIs
- Analyze demand and inventory trends
- Identify operational bottlenecks
- Recommend actionable improvements

Dataset
- 24 months of simulated data (2024–2025)
- Product: Cooking Oil (1L bottle)
- Monthly-level data including:
  - Demand
  - Inventory on hand
  - Orders placed
  - Lead time
  - Stock received
  - Stockout indicator

KPIs Used
- **Inventory Turnover** = Demand / Inventory  
- **Stockout Rate** = Average of stockout occurrences  
- **Average Lead Time** = Mean supplier lead time  
- **Fill Rate** = Stock Received / Demand (capped at 100%)

Dashboard Features
- KPI summary cards (Total Demand, Avg Turnover, Stockout Rate, Lead Time)
- Demand trend analysis (monthly)
- Inventory turnover trend visualization
- Lead time fluctuation tracking
- Stockout occurrence monitoring

Key Insights
- Demand shows consistent seasonal spikes during Nov–Dec  
- Inventory turnover peaks during high-demand periods, indicating tight stock levels  
- Lead time increases during peak months, creating supply delays  
- Stockouts occur primarily when high demand coincides with longer lead times  

Bottlenecks Identified
- Lead time variability during peak periods  
- Poor alignment between demand and replenishment  
- Insufficient inventory before seasonal spikes  
- Reactive rather than proactive procurement planning  

 ecommendations
- Increase safety stock before peak seasons (Nov–Dec)  
- Implement reorder point based on demand and lead time  
- Improve supplier coordination to stabilize lead times  
- Apply demand forecasting (moving average) for better planning  

Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets  
- Data analysis and visualization (charts, KPI metrics)

Outcome
This project demonstrates the ability to transform raw supply chain data into actionable insights, supporting operational efficiency and strategic decision-making.
